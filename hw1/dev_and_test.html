Software Development Life Cycle

SLDC Requirement Analysis & Design Development & Testing Maintenance




Development

  This part of the phases focuses on creating the code. The development process follows the design planned and the requirements that has been mapped. If the past phases were followed and organize this step could be accomplished with ease. Developers follow coding guidelines followed by their organization.


Testing

  After building the code it is tested againsts any requirements and defects from the past phases. Testing involves reports, tracking, fixing, and re-testing methods to figure out solutions.Testing for software defficiency improves the quality of the product for the users.
